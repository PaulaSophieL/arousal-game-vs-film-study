# Privacy Policy for arousal-film-vs-game-study

**Last updated:** 2025-12-01

This privacy statement explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data in the context of the research study **"arousal-film-vs-game-study"** using Garmin wearable devices and the Garmin Health API.

## 1. Data We Collect
With your explicit consent, we collect the following types of data from your Garmin device:
- Heart rate and related metrics
- Heart rate variability (HRV)

No location (GPS) data is collected unless explicitly stated.

## 2. Purpose of Data Processing
Your data is collected **solely for scientific research purposes**, specifically to investigate physiological responses (e.g., arousal, stress, recovery patterns). Your data will **not** be used for commercial purposes.

## 3. Legal Basis
Data processing is based on your **informed consent** in accordance with Art. 6(1)(a) and Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR.

## 5. How Your Data Is Shared
Your data will not be shared with third parties except:
- Garmin, for the purpose of data transfer via the Garmin Health API
- Research collaborators named in the consent form

Data will **never** be sold, monetized, or transferred to advertisers.

## 6. Retention Period
Data will be stored for **1 year** and deleted afterwards, unless you request earlier deletion.

- request access, correction, or deletion of your data
- request restriction of processing
